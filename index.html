Flutter Clock Challenge Submission

A clock written for the Flutter Clock challenge.

The clock is a grid of analog clocks that animate to show a digital time and temperature. The clock repeats 6 minutes of transition animations. The design is inspired by the art work A Million Times by Humans since 1982.

The clock supports the settings: theme, temperature unit and hour format.

This is a beta build of Flutter web with performance not optimal.

GitHub source https://github.com/rt-apps/flutter-clockchallenge
